Rules

Users

General public can see the all photos participating in the contest in the gallery page. If some (of the general public) want to entry a photo in the contest or vote for a particular photo must be registered as a user. As a general user, to vote for a photo, must register its first_name, last_name, email. A participant user, additionally must enter all data required (address, address2[additional address], city, state, zip_code, country, phone).

Photos

To entry a photo in the contest, a participant user, must enter the title, category and description of the photo and then upload the photo, accept terms & conditions, and pay the fee required for each photo. If a general user wants to enter in the contest, must enter all data requires for a participant user, then proceed with photo data, upload the photo, accept the term & conditions, and pay the fee required to participate in the contest. If a fee payment is not processed immediately, the photo will be in stand-by and not show of the photo will appear in gallery. A photo cannot receive votes or be evaluated by the jury until full fee is processed. Photos entered in the contest cannot be changed once the payment is processed. If a participant user wants enter a new photo in the same or other category it must be processed as a new entry. If a participant user wants no longer participate in the contest can remove the photo but, no fee will be reimbursed if payment has been processed.

Votes

A vote can be made for photos appearing in gallery. A general user could choose a photo by category a make the vote. Participants user, is wish, vote for any photo in gallery. A general or participant user can make a vote just for one photo in each category or leave a category with no vote or not made any vote in any category.

Fees

A fee for entre the contest must be made for each photo a participant wants to enroll. In order to participate a photo must have the fee payment processed.

Winning the contest

For a photo to be a winner in all categories or in its category will be evaluated by a jury and all users (general or participants) The judgement of the jury will take professional aspects in the photo evaluation. The final judgement will be the accumulate between jury’s judgment plus total voted accrued being jury’s evaluation 80% and users votes 20%.
